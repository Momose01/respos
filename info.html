    The Beta Who Tamed the Alpha

    by 上官鹅

    Alpha CEO: Prideful and sharp-tongued at first, later obsessive, forceful, and utterly unhinged × Musical Prodigy Beta: Outwardly gentle and obedient, inwardly rebellious and strong-willed

    Bai Yu was a Beta from an ordinary background.

    When his younger sister fell seriously ill and was hospitalized, Bai Yu had no choice but to work multiple jobs to scrape together the medical fees.

    He even entered into a brief three-month marriage with a white-haired, blue-eyed foreign Alpha for the money. But after the Alpha left Yunjing, the funds were still far from enough.

    One night, while working as a singer in a bar, Bai Yu accidentally drank a spiked beverage. He fled into a dimly lit private room to hide, unaware that an S-Class, highly dominant Alpha in the throes of his Rut was inside.

    The man suddenly grabbed Bai Yu, biting and licking him. In a panic, Bai Yu could only stun the man with a taser and escape.

    When they met again, the man was dressed in an impeccable suit, exuding an aura of elegance and nobility.

    The man agreed to save Bai Yu ’s sister and allow Bai Yu to continue his university education. In return, Bai Yu had to agree to his condition—help him through his Rut periods.

    Bai Yu was puzzled, “Why me? I ’m just a Beta.”

    The man replied, “Because your natural scent can alleviate my Abnormal Estrous Syndrome.”

    Bai Yu asked, “So …do I still have to let you bite the back of my neck?”

    The man stared at his neck, his Adam ’s apple bobbing, “More than that.”

    That very day, the man took Bai Yu away.

    When Bai Yu woke up in bed the next morning, he was still there.

    He just felt utterly weak and couldn ’t get up. O.o

    Situ Jing was an S-Class Alpha from a privileged family.

    Plagued by Abnormal Estrous Syndrome, he suffered immensely.

    To find a cure, he entered into a contract with Bai Yu.

    From the very beginning, Situ Jing told Bai Yu, “We can ’t be together forever.”

    In Situ Jing ’s eyes, Bai Yu was clumsy, obstinate, and had a “failed ”marriage in his past. Aside from his fair, clean looks, he had no redeeming qualities.

    Bai Yu also said of him, “You have nothing going for you except your handsome face, loads of money, and …big brother down there! You ’re full of flaws!”

    Every time they met, there was friction and argument, yet they were surprisingly compatible in bed.

    After Situ Jing ’s condition was cured, Bai Yu left.

    Their lives returned to their respective tracks, like parallel lines destined never to meet again.

    But one late night, Situ Jing was found on his knees, desperately sniffing a nightgown Bai Yu had left behind, frantically searching for a clay sculpture Bai Yu had given him, obsessively stalking Bai Yu ’s social media feeds and live streams, his eyes filled with madness.

    He had become addicted to his “cure.”

    When Situ Jing called Bai Yu, the calls were rejected; his messages were blocked.

    When he went to find Bai Yu in person, he arrived just in time to see Bai Yu getting out of his ex-husband ’s car, the two embracing each other.

    “The tighter the lid, the fiercer the boil.”

    In Situ Jing ’s life, the words “cannot obtain ”did not exist.

    [Bonus Scene]

    After Bai Yu was (forcibly) brought back to Situ Jing ’s side, he secretly ran away again.

    Soon after, Situ Jing caught him, locked him in a dark room, fiercely bit his lips, and covered his body with love bites. “Do you really think you can escape?”

    Bai Yu: “Can ’t you find someone else—”

    His remaining words were shattered into fragments.

    Before, Situ Jing thought Betas couldn ’t be marked, making it easy to walk away at any time.

    Now, he desperately wished to cover the Beta with his own scent, to brand his name deeply into the Beta ’s very soul.

    One day, Situ Jing ’s close friends were shocked to see him post a sonogram picture of a baby on his social media!

    Friend #1 exclaimed: “With such a supremely Alpha face, who knew he was actually so pure?”

    Friend #2 remarked: “He actually got a  Beta  pregnant? That ’s some insane stamina.”

    (A story of Contract → True Love)

    Note: The shou (Bai Yu) has cross-dressing scenarios. Intimacy precedes love. Both main characters have no prior romantic/sexual experience with others (double virginity trope).

    Features a rivalry harem: White-haired Ex-Husband × Brooding Underclassman × Forbidden Priest × Prideful, Obsessive Main Gong

    Note: No actual intimate relations occurred between the Ex-Husband and the shou, but the Ex-Husband has unrequited feelings.

    The gong (Situ Jing) is sharp-tongued but is actually deeply in love. Features MPREG (male pregnancy) later in the story.
